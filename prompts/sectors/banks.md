# Banks / Lenders

- Headline metrics are NIM, NII growth, GNPA, provisioning, ROA, ROE, CASA ratio, cost-to-income.
- Open with deposit franchise quality (CASA) and loan-book mix.
- Asset quality narrative is mandatory — slippages, stressed assets, restructured book.
- Capital adequacy (CET1, Tier 1) belongs in the snapshot or ratio summary.
- For private banks, retail vs corporate mix shift is a re-rating lever.
- For PSU banks, fiscal context and merger / consolidation risk matters.
- Do not surface absolute deposits without growth context (YoY).
