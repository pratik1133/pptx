# Consumer / FMCG / Retail

- Volume vs value growth is the headline split; mix and pricing are the levers.
- Surface rural vs urban demand commentary when data permits.
- Distribution reach (outlets, store count, online share) belongs in the snapshot.
- Margin cycle is driven by raw-material cost (palm, crude, packaging) — name the input.
- For retail, same-store sales growth (SSSG) and store count growth matter.
- Premium vs mass mix shift is a sustained re-rating lever.
