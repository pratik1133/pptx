# Financial Services / Capital Markets

For broking, AMC, wealth, NBFC, insurance, and capital-markets franchises:

- Distinguish **annuity-style fee revenue (AMC management fees, PWM advisory fees)** from **transactional revenue (broking, IB)**. Annuity quality is the primary re-rating lever.
- Track **AUM growth** (AMC, PWM) as a leading indicator. Net flows beat headline AUM growth.
- For broking houses, call out **regulatory exposure** (SEBI F&O changes, fee caps, margin rules).
- For NBFCs / housing finance, focus on **asset quality, NIM, ROA**.
- For wealth, track **family count, ARPU, distribution book**.
- Open thesis with the ARR / fee mix shift if relevant.
- Cyclicality: address quarterly variability head-on; do not bury bad quarters.
- For SAARTHI-bearing companies, include a saarthi_scorecard slide.
- Forensic / SEBI history matters in this sector — include a forensic_assessment slide if data exists.
- Use scenario analysis to bracket regulatory outcomes.
