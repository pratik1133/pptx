# Specialty Chemicals / Chemicals

- Lead with **mix shift** (commodity vs specialty) and **export penetration**.
- Margin trajectory is the headline; surface gross margin and EBITDA margin separately when data permits.
- Capacity utilisation and capex cycle drive earnings conversion.
- Working capital intensity matters — flag inventory days and debtor days when ratios available.
- Raw material exposure: name 1–2 key inputs and their volatility risk.
- For diversified chemicals, segment mix is mandatory.
- China supply dynamics and competing imports are a structural risk worth flagging.
