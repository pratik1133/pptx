# Motilal Oswal Financial Services Initiation Note

## Investment View

Motilal Oswal Financial Services is one of the most diversified capital-markets franchises in India, with a structurally improving mix of annuity-style asset management and wealth fees offsetting cyclicality in broking and investment banking. The next leg of growth is being driven by strong client additions, AUM compounding in AMC and PWM, and operating leverage in housing finance.

## Why We Like It

- Wealth and AMC are scaling annuity revenues, reducing earnings volatility.
- Retail broking continues to gain incremental market share among active clients.
- Housing finance has turned the corner on asset quality with disciplined growth.
- Proprietary book provides a credible long-term capital allocation tailwind.
- Operating leverage from a largely fixed cost base supports margin expansion.

## Industry Context

Indian capital-markets franchises continue to benefit from rising household equity allocation, demat additions, and SIP penetration. Wealth and asset-management economics are migrating toward fee-based annuity revenue, narrowing the cyclicality gap with private banks.

## Key Catalysts

- Sustained AMC AUM growth and improvement in industry market share.
- PWM net-new-money momentum and yield resilience.
- Operating leverage as broking technology spend matures.
- Recovery in capital-markets activity boosting investment banking and broking.

## Key Risks

- Cyclical drawdowns in capital-markets activity hurt broking revenue.
- Mark-to-market on the proprietary book can introduce earnings volatility.
- Regulatory tightening on broking pricing or distribution economics.
- Competitive intensity from discount brokers compressing yields.
- Housing finance asset-quality slippage in stressed credit cycles.
